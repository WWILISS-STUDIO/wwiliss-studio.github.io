Behind the music

Made after the shift

WWILISS.STUDIO is an independent Lithuanian music project based in Northern Ireland. The music is created after working hours, shaped by rainy nights, city lights and real-life experiences.

The sound blends cinematic electronic music, dark ambient textures, deep bass and emotional storytelling. Each track begins as a private thought and grows into something meant to reach another person.

“Nobody knows me yet, but the story is still being written.”
